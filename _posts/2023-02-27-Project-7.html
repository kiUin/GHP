THE FINAL: Amblyopia Solver 5000 Design

Amblyopia (“Lazy” Eye) is a common neurodevelopmental deformity that changes the nerve pathways between the retina and the brain, causing impaired vision in one eye and if left untreated, it can lead to permanent vision loss. Today, amblyopia is the most common cause of vision loss in kids, with 3 out of 100 people suffering from it, and I unfortunately am one of those people.

Things to consider when designing:

- Power

- Weight distribution

- Vibrator (4mm is best for its size)
